String Orchestra Instruments.
<h1>String Orchestra Instruments.</h1>

Violin

Good for Melody, and high backing. There are usually 2 sections of violin, violin 1 and 2. It is one octave above Viola.

Viola

Good for backing but not great for melody. It has the same strings as a cello, but is played like a violin. It is one octave aboce Cello and one octave below Violin.

Cello

Good for bass parts and melodies. Very rich in sound. It is one octave below Viola and one octave above Double Bass.

Double Bass

Only used for bass parts. Very deep. It is one octave below cello.
